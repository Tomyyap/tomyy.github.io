About



In addition to the Ciao Table circuit, we are now launching a new initiative called #notalonelyplanet: a world map of “friendly corners”; Places where you can feel free and welcome to start an exchange of greetings or chat with a person next to you. By the end of 2019, we’ll publish this map on our website so that its users, wherever they are, may see if there is a friendly corner nearby.

This is where we need your help as it’s not so easy to find such places. They generally are small bars, restaurants, or shops run by the owners or small parks where people go with their children or their dogs. Even if nobody welcomes you or invites you to communicate, their friendly atmosphere makes you feel that you may address those next to you with no risk of being rebuffed or considered impolite. As they are rare places, we need volunteers for recommendations of these places.

Have you ever felt a welcoming atmosphere in a public area? If so, please, click on this link and let us know so we can add it to our map, which will be published by the end of 2019.

Recommended Place



Palazo Beach

A beach is a landform alongside a body of water which consists of loose particles. The particles composing a beach are typically made from rock, such as sand, gravel, shingle, pebbles. The particles can also be biological in origin, such as mollusc shells or coralline algae.

Danau Desto

Danau Desto is the site of a massive supervolcanic eruption estimated at VEI 8 that occurred 69,000 to 77,000 years ago, representing a climate-changing event. Recent advances in dating methods suggest a more accurate identification of 74,000 years ago as the date.

Meisterstadt Mount

A mountain is a large landform that rises above the surrounding land in a limited area, usually in the form of a peak. A mountain is generally steeper than a hill.
